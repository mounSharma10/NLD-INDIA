DocuVibes

Document Management System (DMS) - DocuVibes, meticulously designed to help you store, organize, and manage your documents securely and efficiently.

Why Docuvibes ?

DocuVibes is equipped with a robust Control Panel, acting as the central administrative hub for managing the system. Accessible only by Super Admins and Admins, this panel houses various modules that enable fine tuning of the DocuVibes environment for each client company, ensuring tailored and secure document management.

Fluentia

Fluentia is an advanced, web-based platform tailored for logistics companies. Designed by Next Level Development (NLD), Fluentia reimagines how businesses handle documents and customs data—offering a seamless, scalable, and secure solution to meet the ever-evolving needs of international logistics operations.

Why Fluentia ?

It allows you to add your store's budget targets and analyse your sales in depth. This system provides comprehensive insights into your store's performance across various categories.
